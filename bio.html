Catalina Cespedes

I am a research specialist in the Phytopathology department in the vegetable seeds division at BASF. I do the development of disease resistance protocols and disease diagnosis for mainly Solanaceous and some Cucurbitaceous plants.

I graduated from my Master degree in plant pathology from Michigan State University. In there I worked with cucurbit Downy mildew for my thesis degree. I also obtained a B.S. and M.Sc. degree in Microbiology
